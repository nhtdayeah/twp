About Us

Our Beginning

The Widaad Project is a social enterprise that runs as a non-profit organisation of which all of our profits will be channeled into our community projects and the costs of running the enterprise. We are made up of a group of youths with diverse backgrounds who have come together to organise community-centred events in a bid to do our part in the society.
Our goal is to make a difference, and at the same time gain new skills through experience and the people we meet. We hope to uplift and bring betterment to our chosen beneficiaries, and to initiate change in their lives so that they can strive to be better than the circumstances that they are in.
